AVAN PLANET

In a quiet corner of the galaxy, a young engineer named Lira built a tiny planet from stardust and dreams. She gave it oceans that shimmered like glass and skies that sang when the wind passed through. But when she looked closer, she saw the world was empty — perfect, yet silent. So one night, she whispered a wish into its heart: “Let there be life.”
